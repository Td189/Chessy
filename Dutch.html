The Dutch Defense

The Dutch Defense is an aggressive yet risky opening for Black which aims to exchange a flank pawn for one of whites valuable central pawns. The risk comes from the exposed diagonal on blacks kingside which can be easily exploited by white if black doesn't defend properly.I strongly suggest studying this weakness if you intend on trying to master the Dutch defense. Regardless the Dutch defense remains a strong opening great for those who enjoy dynamic middle games.
